Fashion Trends

Discover the latest styles and fashion movements that are shaping the industry

Business Core

Business Core

A sophisticated blend of professional attire with contemporary elements, emphasizing tailored suits, structured pieces, and refined accessories that maintain a modern edge while upholding corporate elegance.

Gorpcore

Gorpcore

A fusion of outdoor gear and streetwear, featuring technical outerwear, hiking boots, and performance fabrics that blend functionality with urban aesthetics.

Streetwear

Streetwear

A unique blend of urban aesthetics with modern street fashion, characterized by oversized silhouettes, avant-garde designs, and innovative layering techniques.

Old Money

Old Money

A timeless aesthetic that emphasizes quality, heritage, and understated luxury, featuring classic pieces, neutral colors, and sophisticated accessories that exude quiet wealth.

Y2K

Y2K

A nostalgic revival of early 2000s fashion, featuring bold colors, metallic finishes, low-rise jeans, and playful accessories that capture the essence of the millennium era.

Techwear

Techwear

A futuristic approach to fashion that combines technical functionality with urban aesthetics, featuring waterproof materials, modular designs, and innovative storage solutions.

Workwear

Workwear

A practical and durable style inspired by traditional work clothing, featuring sturdy fabrics, functional details, and utilitarian designs that blend ruggedness with modern fashion.

Bloke Core

Bloke Core

A British-inspired aesthetic that celebrates traditional masculine style with a modern twist, featuring classic tailoring, casual pieces, and a focus on quality craftsmanship.

Minimalism

Minimalism

A refined approach to fashion that emphasizes simplicity, clean lines, and quality over quantity, featuring neutral colors, timeless pieces, and thoughtful design elements.

Opium

Opium

A bold and avant-garde aesthetic that combines luxury with streetwear elements, featuring dramatic silhouettes, rich textures, and a dark, sophisticated color palette.
